      DATA REALITY

      Free does not mean real-time, complete or licensed for redistribution.

      The default lookup uses a documented free global daily-data endpoint with a strict request limit. Indian real-time exchange feeds are a separate licensed product. For a serious result, use current filings or the companion Skill's cited research export.

      IMPORTANT

      This is an educational, personal research project—not a buy suggestion, sell suggestion, target price, advisory service or promise of return. Scores reflect supplied evidence and a personalised philosophy model. They can be wrong, stale or incomplete. Verify filings and consult a properly registered professional when appropriate.

      WHY THIS ARCHITECTURE

      Visual app outside. Research brain inside.

      The app handles animation, scoring transparency, uploads and portfolio maps. The companion Skill handles current web research, primary-source citations and sector judgment, then exports a JSON file this app can import.

      • Alpha Vantage can supply limited free daily BSE data, with a small daily request allowance.
      • It does not guarantee complete Indian fundamentals or free NSE real-time data.
      • NSE's official real-time and delayed data products are licensed and paid.
      • When free data is thin, the honest fallback is a current filing upload—not invented precision.
